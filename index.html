Prioritization Methodology

How We Identified the Top 50 Solo Practitioners

Our scoring system takes into account multiple weighted factors to identify the most promising solo psychiatric practices for Exomind TMS adoption. Each factor contributes to a total score that determines a practice's placement in our prioritized list.

Online Presence

Practices with established websites demonstrate business focus and marketing savvy, suggesting openness to practice-enhancing innovations.

Weight: 25 points

Ratings & Reviews

Higher ratings (4.5+) and substantial review counts indicate established practices with satisfied patients and solid reputations.

Weight: 15 points

Geographic Location

Practices in affluent areas with high concentrations of psychiatrists suggest patients with means to afford treatment.

Weight: 15 points

Depression Treatment Focus

Practices explicitly mentioning depression treatment are prime candidates for TMS technology.

Weight: 10 points
